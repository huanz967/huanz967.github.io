Designer: Grace Huang

Collector: Fatima Al-Jaber

Fatima: "The use of vibrant color in film has become synonymous with contemporary film; so for this idea I wanted to get stills from films made after 1990 and organize them based off of color. All shots will be predominantly the color chosen; and it will be filtered out based on 7 colors.

I have a appreciation for film, especially color in film so having a website that organizes film shots based off of that would be really cool."
